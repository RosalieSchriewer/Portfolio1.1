Avatar builder

The avatar builder is an extension to an already existing application. The owner of this application wished for a way to have their users create an avatar for themselves.
If you want to see the projects code, you can take a look at the GitHub repository. The pictures below show the working and planning process of this app:

First planning

The first planning with the product owner was to establish the general art style of the app and avatar. Here we also decided on the apps functionalities and on the basic interface.

Creating the 3D Models

The character model was made using Blender. A total of 63 types of assets, including clothes, hair and accessories were also created in Blender.

Building an app demo in Figma

The final setup of the app's interface in Figma. As you can see we ended up with a tab layout.

The finished product

The character model and assets are being displayed using three.js. This makes it possible to change colors and rotate the character.
